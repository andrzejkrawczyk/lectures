Gameplay

The player decides whitch card will be played.

Moving a turtle onto an already occupied tile places the turtle on top of the occupant

Moving a turtle with other turtles on top moves them as well.

Card Explanation

There exist rainbow variants of color cards. The player choses which turtle to move when played.

Moves coloured turtle one tile forward
Moves coloured turtle two tiles forward, unless the piece is on the second-to-last tile
Moves coloured turtle one tile backward, unless the piece is on first or start tile
Moves last turtle one tile forward. If there are multpile then player choses one piece.
Moves last turtle two tile forward. If there are multpile then player choses one turtle.

Winning Condition

As soon as one turtle reaches the end, all players uncover their turtle's colour.

The winner is the person whose turtle was the lowest when reaching the end

If an unowned turtle reaches the end, the winning turtle is whichever was the closest to the last tile
